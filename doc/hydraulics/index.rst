Hydraulics documentation
######################

Module description
==================

.. topic:: Overview

    Hydraulics is a turgor-driven model of leaf elongation linking water and C dynamics.
    Water influx is simulated at organ scale based on water potential differences. Water fluxes among organs
    are assumed to occur through a common compartment called *xylem*.
